Welcome!!

Hip-Hop Stroke is a fun and interactive program that teaches kids like you about stroke. Hip Hop Stroke will use music, cartoons, a comic book and a video game to teach you about the signs and symptoms of stroke. This very important information may help you to save someone’s life one day!

Before we get started, we are going to ask you some questions, so that we can find out how well our health education program works. This is a NO STRESS TEST! Your responses to these questions will NOT effect your grades in school!
